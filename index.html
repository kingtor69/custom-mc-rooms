Aspen Therevol's Custom Minecraft Locations


Great for parties, special occasions, or just for fun!

It's a great gift for any Minecrafter in your life,
and all you have to do is:

  1. Choose a template
  2. Choose a featured color
  3. Choose an animal (optional)
  4. Enter a custom message
  5. Enter recipient name as you want it to appear (optional)
  1. Fill out your contact info
  2. Check your email in about a week for a download link
  3. Enjoy the recipient's reaction

At present, these customized spaces only work on PC and Mac versions of Minecraft.
